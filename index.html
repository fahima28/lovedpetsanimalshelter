Welcome to Loved Pets Animal Shelter

We believe every animal deserves a loving forever home.

About Us

At Loved Pets Animal Rescue, we give rescued animals the care and second chance they deserve. Every animal that comes to us is screened for diseases, washed, groomed, and then added to our adoption listings so potential families can meet their new best friend.

We also carefully screen adoptive families for their adoption history, household lifestyle, and commitment to providing a safe, loving home. Our goal is to create lifelong matches that keep pets happy and protected.

Services:

  • Temporary Housing
  • Veterinary Care
  • Behavioral Support

Why We Need Volunteers

Loved Pets depends on volunteers to keep the shelter running. Volunteers help by bathing and grooming animals, playing with pets to keep them social and happy, cleaning kennels and screening rooms, and assisting with office tasks.

If you’d like to make a difference, visit our Contact page to learn how to apply to volunteer.
