Welcome to Loved Pets Animal Shelter

Your support and kindness help us create loving futures for animals in need.

About Us

Loved Pets Animal Shelter is committed to rescuing abandoned, stray, and surrendered animals and helping them find safe, loving homes. We provide temporary housing, food, medical care, and emotional support to every animal that enters our shelter.

Our Services:

  • Temporary Housing
  • Veterinary Care
  • Behavioral Support

Why We Need Volunteers

Volunteers play a vital role in helping our animals feel safe and loved. By offering your time to walk dogs, socialize cats, clean enclosures, and assist with adoption events, you help us provide quality care to every pet in our shelter.
